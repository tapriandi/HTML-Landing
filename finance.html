Frequently asked questions

  • What is Expense AI and how does it work?

    Expense AI is a finance management app designed to help businesses and individuals track and analyze their expenses effortlessly.

    Leveraging advanced AI technology, Expense AI offers features like receipt scanning, detailed expense categorization, multi-currency support, and personalized financial reports.

    You can capture receipts, tag or add notes to expenses, and generate insightful reports to gain a comprehensive understanding of their spending habits.

  • Why should I care about Expense AI?

    At Expense AI, we believe that financial mindfulness starts with understanding spending habits; one can only measure or change what’s being tracked.

    Expense AI simplifies and enhances your financial management by providing accurate and detailed tracking of your expenses. This insight allows for better budgeting, smarter spending decisions, and ultimately, improved financial health.

    Whether you're a business owner or an individual, Expense AI saves you time and effort by automating expense tracking and providing valuable analytics, making financial management effortless and effective.

  • What are the available options to add expenses on Expense AI?

    Expense AI provides several options for adding expenses:

    • Receipt Scanning: Capture every detail of a receipt for accurate, hassle-free expense tracking by scanning and uploading images.
    • PDF Receipts: Upload PDF documents containing multiple expense records directly from the mobile app.
    • Email Receipts: Forward receipts to a dedicated Expense AI email address, which supports auto-forwarding.
    • Manual Entry(WIP): Users can manually enter expense details into the app.
  • Can I try out Expense AI on a Free Trial?

    Yes, you can try Expense AI on a free trial for 7 days. During this period, you can access all features without any charges. After the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid plan, with the price varying based on local currency. For exact pricing details, please consult the respective app store. You can cancel anytime during the trial to avoid any charges.

  • Does Expense AI account for email attachments when creating expenses from email receipts?

    Yes, Expense AI extracts information from both PDF and image attachments of an email. Each attachment is treated as a distinct expense record. If an attachment contains multiple receipts or invoices, Expense AI will detect and create individual expense records for each detail. You can view the files and the extracted expense details directly from the mobile app.

  • Does Expense AI support auto email forwarding?

    Yes, Expense AI supports auto email forwarding. Users can create a dedicated Expense AI email address to forward receipts. With auto-forwarding support, you can ensure all your expense-related emails are automatically processed by Expense AI without manual intervention. We also provide a detailed guide on setting up email forwarding within the app to help you get started smoothly.

  • Can I chat with my Expense data and generate reports of my expenses?

    Yes, Expense AI features an AI chat functionality that allows you to interact with your expense data. You can generate detailed expense reports using natural language commands. For example, you can ask for a report comparing your spending this month to the previous month. Reports can be generated in various formats, including images, CSV, or PDF, and can be customized in terms of style and theme.
